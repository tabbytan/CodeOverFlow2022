Why Join The Fight Against Climate Change?

We are ALL affected, no matter where we live

Climate change won’t just impact forest, or coral reefs, or even people in far-off countries – it will affect all of us. From more extreme weather to increasing food prices, to recreation and decreased opportunities to appreciate the natural world, people everywhere will feel its effects. Reducing our carbon footprint is fundamentally necessary to create a world where people and nature thrive.

Rising Sea Levels

The sea level has increased by 21cm since 1990 and the rate of sea level rising is accelerating. This rise destroy coastal habitat and oust people living along the coastline. Roads, bridges, water supplies, landfills and oil and gas wells remains at risk if the sea level continue to rise.

Ocean Acidification

The ocean absorbs 30% of carbon dioxide released in the atmosphere. In the past 200 years, the pH value of the ocean has decreased by 0.1, this translate to a 30% increase in acidity. Marine life that are unable to adjust to the acidification will die off.
